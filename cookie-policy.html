This Cookie Policy explains how NebulaWeb uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website.

They help websites function properly, improve user experience and provide information about how visitors use the site.

How we use cookies

NebulaWeb may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Help the website function correctly
  • Improve website performance
  • Understand how visitors use the website
  • Analyse traffic and popular pages
  • Improve our content, services and user experience

Types of cookies we may use

Essential cookies

These cookies help the website operate correctly and may be required for basic functionality.

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

We may use tools such as Google Analytics to collect anonymous or aggregated information about website usage.

This may include:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device type
  • Browser type
  • General location data
  • How visitors found the website

Performance cookies

Performance cookies may help us understand how the website performs and identify areas for improvement.

Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services used on our website, such as analytics tools, embedded content, form providers or other website services.

Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to control or block cookies through browser settings.

You can usually:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block some or all cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Please note that blocking some cookies may affect how the website functions.

Google Analytics

If Google Analytics is used on this website, it helps us understand visitor behaviour and improve our website.

You can learn more about Google’s use of data by visiting Google’s own privacy and cookie information pages.

Changes to this Cookie Policy

NebulaWeb may update this Cookie Policy from time to time.

The latest version will be published on this website.
